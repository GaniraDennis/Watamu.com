Meet Coach Viv

Coach Viv is a certified professional swimming coach with over 10 years of experience training beginners, pros, and children in safe, effective swimming techniques along the beautiful Kenyan coast.


With a passion for water safety and aquatic excellence, Coach Viv has helped hundreds of students overcome their fears, master new strokes, and develop confidence in the water. Her personalized approach ensures every swimmer receives the attention they need to succeed.


Based in the stunning coastal town of Watamu, Coach Viv combines professional training with the natural beauty of Kenya's pristine waters to create an unforgettable learning experience.

Swimming Programs

Baby Swimming Classes

Gentle introduction to water for infants and toddlers. Building water confidence from an early age with parent participation.

Adult Beginners

Never too late to learn! Comprehensive beginner program for adults who want to master swimming fundamentals in a supportive environment.

Advanced Stroke Training

Perfect your technique with advanced stroke refinement, competitive training, and performance optimization for serious swimmers.

Water Safety & Lifesaving

Essential water safety skills and lifesaving techniques. Learn to be confident and safe around water while helping others.

Aqua Fitness

Low-impact, high-intensity water workouts that improve cardiovascular health, strength, and flexibility in a fun environment.
